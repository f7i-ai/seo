#### Common Pitfalls in Visual Asset Branding
When implementing the Kubota logo within an [asset management](/features/asset-management) framework, maintenance departments often encounter several common mistakes that can degrade system usability:

1.  **Resolution Mismatch:** Using low-resolution raster files (like small JPEGs) for large-scale equipment signage. This leads to pixelation, which can obscure QR codes or secondary safety warnings integrated into the label. Always use SVG or high-resolution PNG files.
2.  **Color Deviation:** Substituting "Kubota Orange" with a generic orange. In high-stakes environments, color-coding is used for rapid identification. Deviating from Pantone 165C can lead to confusion with other brands (like Husqvarna or certain Caterpillar safety gear), potentially slowing down technician response times.
3.  **Ignoring the "Clear Space" Rule:** Crowding the logo with text or other icons. For maximum legibility on a [mobile CMMS](/features/mobile-cmms) interface, ensure a margin of at least 25% of the logo's height around all sides.
